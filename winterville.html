Winterville Backyard Cinema Clapham, London

Make your way throguh a magical wardrobe and discover an enchanted Christmas labyrinth with live entertainment, a bar and two screens.

The wintery cinema has all the classic Christmas films to get you in the festive mood,as well as singalong blockbusters like the Greatest Showman and Moulin Rouge. There's also a cheeky secret tequilla room - shhhh! In non-festive times, the Backyard Cinema operates in various locations around London.
