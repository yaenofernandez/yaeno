Genesis Cinema Mile End Road

Genesis Cinema is a hip local independent cinema with super reasonable pricing and the option to upgrade to sofa seating in screens 4 and 5.

A rich history dating back to 1848, Genesis Cinema was once a theatre that had Charlie Chaplain perform just before he left for Hollywood. Now the cinema boasts a warm and friendly atmosphere, gorgeous decor and the comfiest seating.Screens 4 and 5 provide for a more luxury experience, but at an affordable price (£9.50), but the £5 seats are also great options. The cinema have a great selections of showings and is perfect for students and film lovers alike.
